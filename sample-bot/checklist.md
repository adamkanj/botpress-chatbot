# Learning Checklist

Use this checklist after you create the sample bot.

- Botpress starts locally.
- You created one bot.
- You added a welcome message.
- You added at least three FAQ answers.
- You created one short guided flow.
- You tested one unknown question and saw fallback behavior.
- You made one change and confirmed it showed up in chat.
- You wrote at least three notes in `docs/work-notes.md`.
